HOW TO PLAY

Basic Controls

  • Move your mouse to control the player's position
  • Bullets fire automatically - no need to click!
  • Collect falling barriers to gain immediate upgrades
  • Choose from different upgrade paths to enhance your abilities

Upgrades

  • Fire Rate: Increases how fast you shoot bullets
  • Damage: Increases the damage of each bullet
  • Bullet Limit: Increases the number of bullets you can fire at once

Boots Upgrade System

  • During gameplay, upgraded boots will occasionally fall from the top of the screen
  • Shoot the falling boots until their health is depleted to upgrade your bullet type
  • If you miss an upgrade opportunity, don't worry! Every 2 bosses defeated, the upgrade boots will reappear

Shop

  • Buy permanent upgrades using coins earned from playing
  • Upgrade bullet limits for different bullet types
  • Increase your maximum health

Coins

  • Earn coins by beat jumps during gameplay:
  • - Regular jumps: 0.1 coins (scales with difficulty)
  • - Boss jumps: 0.3 coins (scales with difficulty)
  • The higher the difficulty, the more coins you earn per enemy!

Important Notes

  • Enemies become stronger as the game progresses
  • You only can change game speed at home page before start
  • Watch out for enemy patterns and adjust your strategy
  • Collecting suitable upgrades is crucial for surviving longer
  • Use the pause button (ll) if you need a break
  • Lower graphics quality if experiencing lag
  • Use the Default button to temporarily lock all bonuses to the initial state (no purchases), click again to restore

Hidden Details

  • There are 6 different bullet types with varying properties
  • Some achievements require specific actions to unlock
  • Game difficulty increases based on your survival time
  • You can reset all upgrades by enabling "Default" mode
  • Bullet bonuses cannot exceed the bullet limit shown in the bottom left corner
  • The probability of bullet bonuses appearing depends on your current bullet limit
  • You can increase your bullet limit by purchasing upgrades in the shop
